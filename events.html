Presents

At Startup Meetup#1 we saw startups with prototypes/strategies from varying domains like logistics, robotics, online communities, business social platforms, Socially Driven Adventure Travel and Trademark & Copyright platform. Nine startups presented on stage, from among these we saw one startup get investor connect and incubation, another startup become an alliance at Ideashacks among others who got connected to take their idea forward. 

Join us in this constructive critique session that promises investor connect to the star products.

Calling all Startup-minded individuals and those who have a Product/Idea to pitch. 

The startups get a platform to practice, refine and perfect their startup pitches/go-to-market strategy. The startups get a chance to discuss their ideas with early stage investors, mentors and peers from the startup ecosystem. 

And the attendees not presenting get a chance to know whats happening in the startup ecosystem and network with like-minded individuals.

Who Can Participate:

• All those with a presentation, prototype, MVP ready for pitching on the platform.

• All who want to know whats happening in the startup ecosystem. 

For Startups who want to present, please fill the form here, and we will shortlist you for the startup line-up.
